Hello world!
This is my go at a digital garden or playground as I like to call it. 
I was inspired by an article by [heise.de](https://www.heise.de/hintergrund/Nerd-Trend-Digitaler-Garten-Die-eigene-Website-als-persoenliches-Wissensarchiv-10344169.html) (in German), which describes the digital garden as a 
way to to collect personal knowledge online and make it available to other users.
What appealed the most to me was the fact that a digital garden is never finished, all projects can be added whatever state they are in.
Publishing them on the internet for potential viewers gives me a sort of external impetus to not abandon my projects.
